Target-audience

This site is for most people. Anybody who is curious about the weather and wanting to see updates can use this website. People who are haeding to work, headed on a trip or just wanting to know can access that information here.

Personas

  • Bobby is a salesman who goes door to door trying to get people to try his product. He is outside most of his workday.
  • Patrick is a cart guy at the local grocery store. His job requires him to work in any occasion.

Scenarios

  • Jayde is a an upcoming photographer and she's planning a halloween shoot for two friends. She needs to know when the best weather will be so she gets good pictures.
  • Paul is a truck driver who is about to head out on a drive. He needs to know the weather so he can plan how long the drive will be. Rainy, stormy weather requires slow driving.
  • May is having a birthday party and wants it to be held outside. Since her birthday is in April there could be any kind of weather.
  • Margaret wants to plant new trees in her backyard but doesn't want it to be raining or windy when she does.
  • Peggy is going on a work trip to Florida but is worried there might be hurricanes or other dangerous weather and might need to reschedule her meetings.
